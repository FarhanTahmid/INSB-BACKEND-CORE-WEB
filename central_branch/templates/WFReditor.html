User Guide

Welcome to the WFReditor! Here's how to make the most of this editor:

  • Undo/Redo: Quickly revert any recent changes with the Undo button, or reapply them using Redo.
  • Text Formatting:
    • Bold: Emphasize your text with the bold option.
    • Italic: Add a stylish slant to your words with italic.
    • Underline: Draw attention by underlining your text.
    • Strikethrough: Cross out text that’s no longer relevant.
  • Text Alignment:
    • Left Align: Align your text to the left margin.
    • Center Align: Center your text on the page.
    • Right Align: Align your text to the right margin.
    • Justify: Evenly distribute text across the width of the page.
  • Lists:
    • Unordered List: Create a bulleted list.
    • Ordered List: Create a numbered list.
  • Indents:
    • Increase Indent: Move your paragraph further from the margin.
    • Decrease Indent: Bring your paragraph closer to the margin.
  • Insert Elements:
    • Insert Image: Add an image via a URL or upload from your computer.
    • Insert Link: Embed a hyperlink in your text.
    • Insert Table: Create and customize tables with rows, columns, and headers.
  • Resize Editor: Expand the editor to full-screen for a more immersive writing experience.

Hover over each button to see a tooltip describing its function. This guide is here to help you explore and use all the features available.
